ABOUT ME

I’m a full-stack web developer from the San Francisco Bay Area. I have a background in graphic design. I have always been creative and had an affinity for the web and how it works. I taught myself HTML back in the days of Netscape Navigator, Yahoo Geocities and AOL. After graduating from college, I went on to become a graphic designer for more than 9 years. While I enjoy graphic design, I felt like there was something missing. I wanted to create products with more interaction with the audience. So I decided to transition into web development.
